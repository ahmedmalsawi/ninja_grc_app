Scoring Reference

Based on the unified report and investigation record. Use this as a guide when scoring cases.

Scoring axes (1-5 each)

Sovereignty / الأثر النظامي: Impact on sovereign regulations (SAMA, ZATCA, PDPL) and local legislation. 1–2: Minor internal procedural breach. 3–4: Breach of material company policy. 5: Clear breach of sovereign regulations or labour law.

Financial / القيمة المالية: Size of actual or potential loss. 1–2: Under 10,000 SAR. 3–4: Between 10,000 and 100,000 SAR. 5: Over 100,000 SAR or involves senior staff.

Evidence / جودة الأدلة: Quality and availability of evidence. 1: Mere allegation or rumour. 2–3: Documents or messages partly support the claim. 4–5: Digital evidence (logs) or conclusive documents.

Reputation / مخاطر السمعة: Reputational risk and senior involvement. 1: Very limited internal impact. 2–3: May affect department or management trust. 4–5: Senior executives involved or risk of public disclosure.

Path (Total score 4-20)

Green (4-8) Preliminary closure / administrative referral.

Yellow (9-14) Restricted follow-up.

Red (15-20) Immediate formal investigation.
