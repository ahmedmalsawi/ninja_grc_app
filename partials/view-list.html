Scoring Reference

Based on the unified report and investigation record. Use this as a guide when scoring cases.

Scoring axes (1-5 each)

Sovereignty: Impact on sovereign regulations (SAMA, ZATCA, PDPL). 1–2: Minor breach. 3–4: Material policy breach. 5: Sovereign/labour law breach.

Financial: 1–2: Under 10k SAR. 3–4: 10k–100k SAR. 5: Over 100k SAR or senior staff.

Evidence: 1: Allegation. 2–3: Partial support. 4–5: Digital/conclusive evidence.

Reputation: 1: Limited impact. 2–3: Department/trust. 4–5: Senior execs or public disclosure risk.

Path (Total 4-20)

Green (4-8) Preliminary closure / administrative referral.

Yellow (9-14) Restricted follow-up; one investigator, exploratory review.

Red (15-20) Immediate formal investigation; Legal Privilege; notify board chair.
